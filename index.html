Zone 2 Trainer

Fitness coaching app

A heart-rate-based endurance training product focused on helping users understand zone efficiency, recovery signals, and personalized workout guidance.

  • Problem: endurance training data is often scattered across activity logs, heart-rate charts, and generic plans.
  • Solution: consolidate activity context into a coaching workflow centered on zone 2 training and recovery-aware guidance.
  • Execution: Android application with Strava API integration and Firebase-backed iteration. Submitted to Google Play — currently in review.
